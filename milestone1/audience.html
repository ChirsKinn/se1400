Audience

Geolocation

The location of the audience will only be Colorado residence. It will also be focused on Colorado Springs. This will mean that images and content can be specific to the area, and scenery of the city of Colorado Springs.

Age

The age primarily will be those over 20 and upwards of 60+, however the ages that need this specific lawfirm will vary widely. The main focus will be those in their 40's and up.

Gender

The site will cater to all genders, however it should be noted that a majority of the customers are males.

Other

Generally speaking it is important for the website to be very easy to use, as the customers will most likely not be tech-savy. It will also need to be easily accesible, with many different options to access the site such as mobile and other means. Many customers will not have access to a computer when going onto the website.
