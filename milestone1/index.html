Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

This site will contain a function where you can submit a picture, and it will turn the picture into pixel art.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will make the content for the site, including the bot, and I will also include pictures that have been pixelated as examples on the site.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

This is a very unique, and cool site that people will enjoy to use. They can turn any of their pictures into cool works of art!

Why should a site be built for this?

Pixel art is cool, but time consuming. Something like this would enable anyone to make cool pixel art from anything.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to be able to upload pictures, and recieve a pixelated version of their picture.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal is to allow anyone to upload pictures to the site, and get a pixelated version in return of that same picture.
